SamadhanPortal - Product Vision
Vision: To provide instant, reliable, and affordable access to verified personal helpers, enabling people to manage daily life tasks efficiently and stress-free.

Positive Changes it Will Bring:

  • Provide instant access to help
  • Reduce stress from daily tasks
  • Enable efficient time management

Positive Changes it Will Bring:

  • Alleviate anxiety over finding trusted help
  • Provide instant access to help
  • Enable efficient time management
🏠 Target Group
Market segment:
  • Urban & semi-urban households
  • Working professionals
  • Elderly individuals
  • Students
Customers:
  • Busy professionals (22-45)
  • Families
  • Elderly people
🛡️ Needs / Problems
Problems:
  • Difficulty finding trusted helpers
  • No quick solution for urgent tasks
  • Lack of verified workers
Need:
  • On-demand reliable service
Benefits:
  • Saves time
  • Ensures safety
  • Convenience
Willingness to Pay:
  • Yes - acceptance of service fees
⚙️ Product
What is the product?
  • Mobile responsive website for booking helpers (App planned for future)
  • Real-time tracking of helpers
  • Secure payment integration
What makes it special?
  • AI-based task description suggestion
  • Focus on small, urgent tasks
  • Verified & rated workers
Is it viable to build?
  • Yes - leverages modern web tech stack (React/Next.js, Firebase), scalable and cost-efficient
📈 Business Goals
Benefit to the company:
  • Commission per booking
  • Subscription model for frequent users
  • Entry into the on-demand helper market
Business goals:
  • 1. Achieve 10,000 users in 6 months.
  • 2. Expand service to 10 tier-1 cities.
  • 3. Build a trusted ecosystem of helpers and users.
